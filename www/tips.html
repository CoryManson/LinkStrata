What we do

LINK Strata Management (LINK) recognises the increasingly complex demands and time constraints placed
on the members of Executive Committees and the desire for a solution to the issues that typically
concern the owners and Committee Members of Owners Corporations.

LINK assists Owners Corporations by providing access to timely and professional administration, management,
maintenance and compliance resources in a cost effective structure. LINK will relieve Committee Members of
many of the frustrations they currently face, in order that they may more fully enjoy the benefits
of property ownership.

Please be advised that we do not undertake sales or property management functions therefore ensuring we can provide independent advice to our Owners Corporations.

Accounting and Financial
LINK is based on an accounting practice and provides prompt and efficient service from highly skilled and
trained staff. Some of the items we take care of are below:
• Provide monthly statutory reconciled accounts to our clients via our Owners Portal
• Work with the Executive Committee to prepare the Administrative Fund Budget and Sinking Fund Budget
• Issue levy notices
• Levy arrears management
• Manage administrative funds and sinking funds
• Business Activity Statement preparation and lodgement where required
• Pay invoices on behalf of our clients
• Invest monies with authorised deposit taking institutions
• Prepare and issue section 119 certificates
• Arrange for preparation and lodgement of tax returns
• Arrange for audits to be conducted where required

Maintenance and Repairs
Prompt action on any maintenance is crucial to protecting the value of your complex, and we treat it with
absolute priority. Communication and transparency are the key in any working relationship and therefore
no repairs are undertaken without the approval of the Owners Corporation Executive Committee.
We arrange repairs and maintenance, including quotes, so we make sure everything is done professionally
and cost-effectively.

If you have a preferred contractor, that is ok by us! So long as they are appropriately insured and
qualified, we are happy to work with any existing contractors at your complex. Whilst we have a
network of contractors that can assist, we do not collect any commission or commercial benefits
from them.
Records Management
It is critical that the records of your Units Plan are looked after and kept safe. Therefore our systems
have been established to allow us to keep your records with complete accuracy and detail.

We ensure this is the case by:
• Accurately prepare and maintain minutes of meetings
• Maintaining the minute book for your complex
• Maintaining your correspondence file
• Recording and maintaining notices under the applicable sections of the appropriate legislation
• Maintaining the corporate register
• Keeping plans of your complex secure and safe
• Maintaining the common seal
• Preparing and issuing Section 119 certificates
Meetings
We work with the Executive Committee to ensure all meetings are held at a suitable time and location
for all those attending meetings.
LINK works hard before, during and after meetings to ensure they run as smoothly as possible and
owners can make informed decisions. We also follow through on all meeting tasks
promptly and communicate this back to the Executive Committee.
Before, during and after meetings we:
• Communicate with the Executive Committee about your preferred time and location of meetings
• Prepare Meeting Papers including budgets (where required) and proxy forms
• Receive Proxy/absentee voting papers and ensure all members present are entitled to vote
• Take minutes of the meeting and assist the meeting to go smoothly
• Get the minutes of the meeting approved by the Chairperson and distribute to owners
• Action any outcomes as required from the meeting
Insurance

We ensure that your complex is correctly insured as per the Unit Titles (Management) Act 2011.
We are not an insurance company but we can assist you by oversighting claims, obtain valuations,
submit quotations to the Executive Committee, and renew insurances with the Committee’s approval.
